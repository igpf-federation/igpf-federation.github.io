Booking an appointment

Call your GP 8am to 8pm, seven days a week and ask for an I:HUB appointment.

If your surgery is open when you call you will speak to one of your usual receptionists. If closed your call will be diverted to an I:HUB call handler who will help you with your needs.

If you do not remember your GP surgery name or telephone number use our GP finder below.
