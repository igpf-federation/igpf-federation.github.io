Contact Us

For general inquiries about I:HUB Islington GP hubs please email the team at islingtonGP@nhs.net.

Alternatively, you can provide feedback by calling our Patient Advice & Liaison Line on 0203 859 4959. The phone lines are open between 12 noon and 2pm each weekday. Please note there is no answer phone facility.

For further information...

If you would like to find out more about extended hours GP services or other aspects of Primary Care in Islington you may find the following links useful:

NHS's Head of Primary Care Commissioning on seven day services: https://www.england.nhs.uk/2013/10/david-geddes/

Improving general practice – a call to action: https://www.england.nhs.uk/ourwork/qual-clin-lead/calltoaction/igp-cta/

NHS Five Year Forward View: https://www.england.nhs.uk/ourwork/futurenhs/

Information about healthcare in Islington: http://www.healthwatchislington.co.uk/
